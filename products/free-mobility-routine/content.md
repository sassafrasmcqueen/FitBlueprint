# 5-Minute Morning Mobility Routine
## Wake Up Your Body, Boost Your Energy

Start your day with this quick routine to improve flexibility, reduce stiffness, and prepare your body for movement.

### The Routine (60 seconds per move)

1. **Neck Circles:** Gently roll your head in a circular motion. 30s clockwise, 30s counter-clockwise.
2. **Shoulder Rolls:** Roll your shoulders up, back, and down. Focus on opening your chest.
3. **Cat-Cow Stretch:** On hands and knees, arch your back towards the ceiling (Cat), then drop your belly and look up (Cow).
4. **World's Greatest Stretch:** From a lunge position, place the opposite hand on the floor and rotate your other arm towards the sky. Switch sides after 30s.
5. **Bodyweight Squats:** Slow and controlled squats to wake up your lower body and joints.

### Benefits
- Increases blood flow to muscles.
- Improves joint range of motion.
- Reduces morning stiffness and "brain fog".
