# 30-Day Plank Challenge
## Build a Rock-Solid Core in Just Minutes a Day

### Introduction
The plank is one of the most effective exercises for building core strength and stability. This 30-day challenge is designed to gradually increase your hold time, taking you from beginner to core-strength master.

### The Challenge Schedule
- **Days 1-5:** 20 seconds -> 30 seconds -> 40 seconds -> 45 seconds -> Rest
- **Days 6-10:** 45 seconds -> 55 seconds -> 60 seconds -> 60 seconds -> Rest
- **Days 11-15:** 60 seconds -> 70 seconds -> 80 seconds -> 90 seconds -> Rest
- **Days 16-20:** 90 seconds -> 100 seconds -> 105 seconds -> 120 seconds -> Rest
- **Days 21-25:** 120 seconds -> 130 seconds -> 135 seconds -> 140 seconds -> Rest
- **Days 26-30:** 150 seconds -> 160 seconds -> 170 seconds -> 180 seconds -> 5 MINUTE CHALLENGE!

### Plank Variations
- **Forearm Plank:** The standard version.
- **Side Plank:** Focus on obliques and lateral stability.
- **High Plank:** Arms extended (push-up position).
- **Plank Jacks:** Add a cardio element by jumping your feet out and in.

### Form Tips
- Keep your back flat. Don't let your hips sag or hike up.
- Engage your glutes and quads.
- Keep your neck neutral (look at the floor).
