# 7-Day Meal Prep Guide & Grocery List
## Eat Better, Save Time, Feel Great

### Introduction
Meal prepping is the secret to staying on track with your nutrition. This 7-day guide provides a simple, delicious, and balanced menu to fuel your week.

### Grocery List
#### Produce
- 2 bags Spinach/Kale mix
- 1 bag Sweet Potatoes
- 1 Broccoli head
- 2 Bell Peppers
- 1 pack Blueberries
- 3 Bananas

#### Protein
- 1.5 lbs Chicken Breast
- 1 pack Lean Ground Turkey
- 1 carton Eggs
- 1 tub Greek Yogurt (Plain)

#### Pantry/Grains
- Quinoa or Brown Rice
- Olive Oil
- Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder, Paprika
- Almonds or Walnuts

### The Menu
- **Breakfast:** Overnight Oats with Blueberries or Scrambled Eggs with Spinach.
- **Lunch:** Roasted Chicken with Sweet Potato and Broccoli.
- **Dinner:** Turkey Stir-fry with Bell Peppers and Brown Rice.
- **Snack:** Greek Yogurt with a handful of nuts.

### Prep Instructions (Sunday Prep)
1. **Grains:** Cook a large batch of quinoa or brown rice.
2. **Proteins:** Roast the chicken breast and brown the ground turkey.
3. **Veg:** Roast sweet potatoes and broccoli in the oven at 400°F for 25 mins. Chop bell peppers.
4. **Assemble:** Portion out lunches into airtight containers.
