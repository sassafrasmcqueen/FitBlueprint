# Yoga for Beginners: 4-Week Foundation Program
## Build Your Practice from the Ground Up

### Introduction
Discover the transformative power of yoga with our structured 4-week foundation program. Whether you want to increase flexibility, reduce stress, or build strength, this program provides everything you need to start your yoga journey with confidence.

### Weekly Themes
- **Week 1: Foundations & Breath.** Focus on alignment and connecting breath with movement.
- **Week 2: Strength & Stability.** Building power in the legs and core.
- **Week 3: Flexibility & Flow.** Moving through sequences with ease and grace.
- **Week 4: Mind-Body Integration.** Combining all elements for a complete practice.

### Practice Schedule (3-4 times per week)
1. **Mountain Pose (Tadasana):** The blueprint for all standing poses.
2. **Sun Salutation A:** A classic sequence to warm up the body.
3. **Warrior I, II, & III:** Essential poses for building focus and strength.
4. **Tree Pose:** Improve your balance and concentration.
5. **Final Relaxation (Savasana):** Integrating the benefits of your practice.

### What's Included
- Detailed pose descriptions and alignment tips.
- Weekly workout schedules.
- Breathing exercises (Pranayama) for stress relief.
