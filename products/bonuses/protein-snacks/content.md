# Top 10 High-Protein Snacks
## Quick & Easy Fuel for Muscle Recovery

Keep these on hand to curb hunger and hit your protein targets!

1. **Greek Yogurt (Plain):** 15-20g protein. Top with berries.
2. **Hard-Boiled Eggs:** 6g protein per egg. Pre-make for the week.
3. **Cottage Cheese:** 12-15g protein. Eat with sliced cucumber or pineapple.
4. **Beef Jerky (Lean):** 9-12g protein per serving. Great for on-the-go.
5. **Edamame (Steamed):** 8g protein per half cup. Season with sea salt.
6. **Protein Shake:** 20-30g protein. Mix with water or unsweetened almond milk.
7. **Canned Tuna (In Water):** 20g protein per tin. Mix with a little mustard.
8. **Almonds:** 6g protein per handful. Also high in healthy fats.
9. **Roasted Chickpeas:** 7g protein per half cup. Crunchy and satisfying.
10. **Nut Butter on Celery:** 4-7g protein. Use natural almond or peanut butter.
