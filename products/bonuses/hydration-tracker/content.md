# Daily Hydration Tracker
## Stay Hydrated, Stay Healthy

Hydration is essential for energy, recovery, and overall wellness. Aim for at least 8-10 glasses (2-2.5 liters) of water daily.

### Quick Tips
- Carry a reusable water bottle everywhere.
- Set reminders on your phone or use an app.
- Drink a glass of water immediately after waking up.
- Add a slice of lemon or cucumber for flavor.
