# 4-Week Beginner Bodyweight Workout Plan
## Get Fit at Home - No Equipment Needed

### Introduction
Welcome to your 4-week journey to a stronger, healthier you! This plan is designed specifically for beginners who want to build a solid foundation of fitness without needing a gym membership or expensive equipment.

### Weekly Schedule
- **Monday:** Full Body Workout A
- **Tuesday:** Active Recovery (Walking/Yoga)
- **Wednesday:** Full Body Workout B
- **Thursday:** Active Recovery (Walking/Yoga)
- **Friday:** Full Body Workout A
- **Saturday:** Light Activity or Rest
- **Sunday:** Rest

### Exercises
#### Full Body Workout A
1. **Bodyweight Squats:** 3 sets of 10-12 reps
2. **Push-ups (or Knee Push-ups):** 3 sets of 8-10 reps
3. **Lunges:** 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
4. **Plank:** 3 sets of 20-30 seconds
5. **Jumping Jacks:** 3 sets of 30 seconds

#### Full Body Workout B
1. **Glute Bridges:** 3 sets of 12-15 reps
2. **Mountain Climbers:** 3 sets of 20 seconds
3. **Bird-Dog:** 3 sets of 10 reps per side
4. **Superman:** 3 sets of 10 reps
5. **High Knees:** 3 sets of 30 seconds

### Progression
- **Week 1:** Focus on form.
- **Week 2:** Add 2 reps to each set.
- **Week 3:** Reduce rest time between sets by 15 seconds.
- **Week 4:** Add 1 set to each exercise.
