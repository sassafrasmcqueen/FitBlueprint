# Monthly Habit Tracker
## Consistency is the Key to Success

### How to Use
1. **Choose 5-8 habits** you want to cultivate (e.g., Drink 2L water, 10k steps, Meditate 10 mins).
2. **Write them** in the left column.
3. **Cross off or color in** the circle for each day you successfully complete the habit.
4. **Reflect** at the end of the month on your progress.
