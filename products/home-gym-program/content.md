# 12-Week Home Gym Program
## Level Up Your Strength at Home

### Introduction
Ready to move past the basics? This 12-week program is designed for intermediate trainees with access to minimal equipment (dumbbells or kettlebells and a bench). We use progressive overload to ensure you keep getting stronger.

### Equipment Needed
- A set of adjustable dumbbells or a selection of kettlebells.
- A sturdy bench or chair.
- Resistance bands (optional but recommended).

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)
Focus on perfect form and consistent tempo.
- **Workout A:** Goblet Squats, DB Bench Press, DB Rows, DB Overhead Press.
- **Workout B:** Romanian Deadlifts, Push-ups, Lunges, Plank.

### Phase 2: Hypertrophy (Weeks 5-8)
Increase volume and decrease rest time.
- Adding isolation movements (Lateral Raises, Bicep Curls, Tricep Extensions).

### Phase 3: Strength & Power (Weeks 9-12)
Increase weight/resistance and focus on explosive movement.

### Tracking Progress
Keep a log of every set, rep, and the weight used. Your goal is to beat your "previous self" every single week.
