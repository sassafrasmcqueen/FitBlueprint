# Bodyweight Leg Day Workout
## Stronger Legs, Anywhere, Anytime

### Introduction
You don't need a heavy squat rack to build strong, powerful legs. This equipment-free workout targets your quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves with effective bodyweight movements.

### The Workout (3 Difficulty Levels)

#### Level 1: Beginner
- **Air Squats:** 3 sets of 12 reps.
- **Glute Bridges:** 3 sets of 15 reps.
- **Alternating Lunges:** 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.
- **Calf Raises:** 3 sets of 15 reps.
- Rest 60s between sets.

#### Level 2: Intermediate
- **Jump Squats:** 3 sets of 10 reps.
- **Single-Leg Glute Bridges:** 3 sets of 12 reps per leg.
- **Bulgarian Split Squats:** 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.
- **Lateral Lunges:** 3 sets of 12 reps per leg.
- Rest 45s between sets.

#### Level 3: Advanced
- **Pistol Squats (Assisted if needed):** 3 sets of 5-8 reps per leg.
- **Glute Bridge March:** 3 sets of 20 reps total.
- **Explosive Switch Lunges:** 3 sets of 20 reps total.
- **Wall Sit:** 3 sets to failure (aim for 60s+).
- Rest 30s between sets.

### Keys to Success
- **Tempo:** Focus on slow, controlled movements, especially on the way down.
- **Mind-Muscle Connection:** Squeeze your glutes and quads at the top of every rep.
- **Full Range of Motion:** Get low on your squats and lunges.
