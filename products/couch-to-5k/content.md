# Couch to 5K: 8-Week Training Plan
## Your Journey from the Sofa to the Finish Line

### Introduction
The Couch to 5K (C25K) program is a tried-and-tested method for helping beginners become runners. It uses intervals of walking and running to build your cardiovascular fitness and leg strength gradually, minimizing the risk of injury.

### Weekly Structure
Each week consists of 3 workout days with rest or light activity days in between.

- **Week 1:** 5 min warm-up walk, then 60s run / 90s walk (8 intervals), 5 min cool-down.
- **Week 2:** 5 min warm-up walk, then 90s run / 2 min walk (6 intervals), 5 min cool-down.
- **Week 3:** 5 min warm-up walk, then 2 x (90s run/90s walk, 3 min run/3 min walk), 5 min cool-down.
- **Week 4:** 5 min warm-up walk, then 3 min run, 90s walk, 5 min run, 2.5 min walk, 3 min run, 90s walk, 5 min run, 5 min cool-down.
- **Week 5:** Focus on longer continuous runs (up to 20 mins by the end of the week).
- **Week 6:** Re-introducing intervals but with longer run times (up to 25 mins total).
- **Week 7:** 25 min continuous run (3 sessions).
- **Week 8:** 28 min run -> 30 min run -> 5K Goal!

### Essential Running Tips
1. **Proper Footwear:** Invest in quality running shoes.
2. **Slow Down:** You should be able to hold a conversation while running.
3. **Listen to Your Joints:** If something hurts (not just muscle soreness), rest.
4. **Hydrate:** Drink water throughout the day, not just during your run.
5. **Warm Up/Cool Down:** Never skip these 5-minute sessions.
