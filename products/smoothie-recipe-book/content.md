# Healthy Smoothie Recipe Book
## 25+ Nutritious Recipes for Every Goal

### Introduction
Smoothies are a convenient and delicious way to pack in nutrients. This book features over 25 recipes designed to fuel your energy, speed up recovery, and support your health goals.

### Recipes by Goal

#### Energy & Focus
- **Morning Glow:** Spinach, green apple, ginger, and lemon.
- **Matcha Magic:** Matcha powder, banana, almond milk, and honey.
- **Tropical Power:** Pineapple, mango, coconut water, and a scoop of protein.

#### Post-Workout Recovery
- **Berry Blast:** Mixed berries, Greek yogurt, and flax seeds.
- **PB & J Protein:** Peanut butter, strawberries, oat milk, and vanilla protein.
- **Chocolate Cherry:** Frozen cherries, cocoa powder, and hemp seeds.

#### Weight Loss & Detox
- **Green Goddess:** Kale, cucumber, celery, and lime.
- **Clean Slate:** Pear, cucumber, parsley, and coconut water.
- **Zesty Beet:** Roasted beet, orange, and ginger.

### Tips for the Perfect Smoothie
1. **Liquid First:** Add your liquid base first to help the blender move.
2. **Frozen Fruit:** Use frozen fruit for a thicker, creamier texture without ice.
3. **Add Healthy Fats:** Avocado, nuts, or seeds keep you full longer.
4. **Boost It:** Add superfoods like chia, hemp, or spirulina for extra nutrients.
