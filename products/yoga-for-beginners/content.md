# 14-Day Yoga for Beginners Challenge
## Find Your Balance, Strength, and Peace

### Introduction
Yoga is more than just stretching; it's a practice for both body and mind. This 14-day challenge is designed for absolute beginners. We'll start slow, focusing on breath and basic postures, and gradually build your confidence.

### The 14-Day Schedule
- **Day 1:** Introduction to Breath & Mountain Pose (Tadasana)
- **Day 2:** Gentle Forward Folds & Hamstring Stretches
- **Day 3:** Warrior I & Building Leg Strength
- **Day 4:** Warrior II & Opening the Hips
- **Day 5:** Balance Basics: Tree Pose (Vrksasana)
- **Day 6:** Gentle Twists for Spinal Health
- **Day 7:** Rest & Gentle Stretching (Child's Pose Focus)
- **Day 8:** Sun Salutation (Surya Namaskar) A - Step by Step
- **Day 9:** Core Stability with Plank & Boat Pose
- **Day 10:** Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana) Alignment
- **Day 11:** Strengthening the Back with Cobra Pose
- **Day 12:** Heart Openers: Bridge Pose
- **Day 13:** Putting it All Together: Full Sun Salutation
- **Day 14:** Celebration & Final Relaxation (Savasana)

### Key Poses for Beginners
1. **Mountain Pose (Tadasana):** The foundation of all standing poses.
2. **Downward-Facing Dog:** A full-body stretch and strengthening pose.
3. **Warrior I & II:** For building power and focus.
4. **Child's Pose (Balasana):** Your go-to resting posture.
5. **Tree Pose:** To improve balance and concentration.

### Tips for Success
- **Listen to your body:** Never push into pain.
- **Consistency over intensity:** Even 10 minutes a day makes a difference.
- **Focus on the breath:** Breathe deeply and through your nose.
